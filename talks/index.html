Conference Talks

At The Frontend 2016

I gave a talk at together with my collegue Søren Vind at At The Frontend. See the talk description below. It's available on Vimeo.

At issuu we strongly believe that the only way to figure out if we are shipping a good product is to let our users tell us through their actions. Only by learning through data can we improve our product and user experience. Nobody would ever deploy a backend service used by millions and not invest in measuring, monitoring and tracking user behaviour, performance and errors. Yet when the code runs on the browser, this is not unheard of. In this presentation we’ll focus on what, how, and why we’re measuring various metrics related to performance, quality, and feature usage in our javascript client code-base and how it helps us build a better product.
